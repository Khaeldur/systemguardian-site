Overview

System Guardian is a macOS system monitoring application built with your privacy as the foremost priority. The app collects no personal data whatsoever.

Effective date: February 15, 2026

No Data Collection

System Guardian does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No telemetry of any kind
  • No network connections are made by the app
  • No crash reports are sent externally

On-Device Only

All system monitoring data — including CPU, memory, disk, and network statistics — stays entirely on your device. Nothing leaves your Mac.

First-Party Frameworks Only

System Guardian is built exclusively with Apple first-party frameworks. There are no third-party libraries, SDKs, or services embedded in the application.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers. Your system data is never shared with anyone.
